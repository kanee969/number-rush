The Game does not require you to create an account or enter personal information such as your name or phone number. Device information: device model, operating system and version, language settings, and device identifiers.

Usage and interaction data: screens you visit, session duration, and in-game events.
Crash diagnostics: crash logs and performance information.
Ad interaction data: ad impressions and clicks.
